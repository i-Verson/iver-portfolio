MindScape

MindScape is a Mental Health Awareness System for the College students of the University of Perpetual Help System Dalta. It focuses on helping the students to be aware of their mental health condition, also the system has some features like chatbot, appointment booking, freedom wall, live messaging, video call and mental health resources, and it is developed using Laravel, PHP, Tailwind CSS, CSS, and MySQL.
